Privacy Policy

Finance Tracker · Last updated 23 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Finance Tracker app ("the app", "we", "us") — built by Lyvorian Studio — collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By creating an account and using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information you provide

  • Account details — a username and password (your password is stored only as a securely hashed value, never in plain text). Optionally an email address, used only for password reset.
  • Financial data you enter — expenses, income, budgets, goals, bills, accounts, categories and notes that you choose to record. This is personal budgeting data; the app does not ask for or store bank account numbers, card numbers, or payment credentials.
  • Convenience sign-in — if you set them up, a numeric PIN and/or a device passkey/biometric credential to make signing in faster. Biometric data itself (fingerprint, face) never leaves your device — it is verified by Android and we only receive a yes/no result.
  • Pro subscription payments — if you upgrade to Finance Pro, payment is handled entirely by Razorpay; we do not receive or store your card, UPI, or bank details. We only receive a subscription ID and payment status from Razorpay to activate Pro features on your account.

2. Information collected automatically

  • Session & security data — a login session cookie and basic technical data (such as your IP address) used to keep you signed in and to rate-limit sign-in attempts.
  • Advertising (Google AdMob) — the app displays banner ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and related device information to serve and measure ads. See Google's policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

3. Camera and notification access (optional features)

  • Camera — bill/receipt scanning. If you use the "Scan Bill" feature, the app reads text from a photo you take or choose entirely on your device — no photo is uploaded to our servers. Only the amount, vendor, category, and date you review and confirm are saved to your account; the photo itself is discarded afterward.
  • Camera — QR/barcode scanning. Used only to read a QR or barcode code you point your camera at. The camera feed is not recorded or transmitted anywhere.
  • Notification access — UPI payment auto-detect (Android app, optional, off by default). If you turn this on in Settings, the app reads the text of bank/UPI payment notifications on your device to detect the amount and offer to log it as an expense. This uses Android's Notification Listener permission — the app never reads your SMS messages. All detection happens on your device; the raw notification text is never sent to our servers. Only the expense entry you explicitly confirm (amount, category, description) is saved to your account, the same as an expense you'd type in yourself. You can turn this off at any time in Settings, which also revokes the app's notification access.

4. How your information is used

  • To provide the core service — storing and showing the financial data you enter.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To send a password-reset email if you request one.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the app free.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your financial entries for advertising or share them with advertisers.

5. Data sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties except:

  • Google AdMob, for the advertising described above.
  • Razorpay, to process Finance Pro subscription payments. Razorpay receives your payment details directly and shares back only a subscription ID and payment status — see Razorpay's privacy policy.
  • Brevo, our transactional email provider, to send account-verification, password-reset, and (only if you use the "email me a backup" feature) backup-file emails. Brevo only sees the recipient address and content of that specific email.
  • Our hosting provider (Railway), which stores the app's database and runs its servers on our behalf.
  • Where required by law.

6. Data storage & security

Your data is stored in the app's database on our hosting provider's servers. All traffic between the app and the server is encrypted in transit using HTTPS, and passwords are stored as salted hashes. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can delete your data at any time from within the app, or request full account and data deletion by contacting us at the address below. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

8. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Your choices

  • You can reset your device's advertising ID or opt out of ad personalisation in your Android settings (Settings → Google → Ads).
  • You can edit or delete individual entries, or your whole account, at any time.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at: support@lyvorianstudio.co.in
